Conditions for Paid Spectre (Clinical) Support

Last updated: 11 Jun 2015

All the code on this web site is free to use. You can make both commercial and non-commercial use of it without paying anything.

If you are a CRO or a pharmaceutical company with perhaps 20 or more Clinical reporting SAS programmers working for you then you might consider it worthwhile to take out a support contract to give you help and advise with these macros and to have bugs fixed and enhancements made. The cost of this is shown on the download page.
